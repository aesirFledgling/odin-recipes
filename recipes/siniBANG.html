SiniBANG

Description

Maybe you've heard of Sinigang from your Filipino friends. It is a delectable soup or stew with its distinct acidic, savory taste. This one is almost same but with an intentional kick.

SiniBANG is not recommended for minors and pregnant women. Extreme caution is required.

Ingredients:

  • Meat 1 kg.
  • Tamarind 1 kg.
  • Onions (Chopped)
  • Water Spinach
  • Okra 1 bundle
  • Tomatoes
  • Taro
  • Fish Sauce
  • Bottled Water Rapids (Yes)
  • Latin Bible
  • Talisman Paper (Optional)
  • Extra Virgin Blood (Optional with Talisman Paper)

Steps:

  1. Start boiling choice meat, onions, taro,, tomatoes and tamarind in water rapids for 1 hour
  2. Fish out the tamarind and tomatoes, mash them in a bowl
  3. DO NOT remove scum as it will be purified later
  4. Add mashed tamarind and tomatoes
  5. DO NOT stir as it will mess up the rapids' natural flow
  6. Add a dash of fish sauce
  7. Read a Latin verse to calm rapids a bit
  8. If you accidentally summoned a demon, use the extra virgin blood to write seals on the talisman paper
  9. If the sealing talismans fail, read a Latin verse to exorcise demon out of existence
  10. If you accidentally summoned another demon, DO NOT fight. Spray them with fish sauce if you still have some left
  11. Now that those demons are experiencing a nasal hell, throw okra in the pot
  12. Garnish with two handfuls of water spinach
  13. Set aside for 5 minutes and serve
