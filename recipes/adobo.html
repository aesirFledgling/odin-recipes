Adobo

Description

The Philippine Chicken Adobo is an all-time favorite of Filipinos and even foreigners who have been to the Philippines for quite some time.

Adobo is one of the Philippines' easiest dish to make. Although the recipe varies depending on the region, Adobo is Adobo and you too, will love every version.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Thighs 1/2 kg.
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Onion (diced)
  • Peppercorn
  • Bay Leaves

Steps:

  1. Saute garlic and onion until golden-brown
  2. Then the chicken to the mix and stir around until meat turns white-ish
  3. Add about 1 cup of soy sauce to the mix
  4. Once the meat takes on the color of the soy sauce, add about 1/4 cup of vinegar
  5. Let it simmer for a minute or two then add 1-2 cups of water
  6. Throw in a pinch or two of peppercorn and 2 bay leaves
  7. Cover and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes
  8. Salt to taste
