BADobo

Description

A variety of Adobo from the Philippines made not only adding a unique kick but also the most brutal beating of your life.

Made with pork carved out of real BADass hogs from the baddest place in the Philippines. Guaranteed to give you high blood pressure and an acute heart attack in as early as 3 minutes.

What you need:

  • Pork (from Tondo)
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic (fist-pounded)
  • Onion (karate-chopped)
  • Peppercorn
  • Bay Leaves
  • Last Will and Testament
  • Somebody to call 911

Steps:

  1. Boil the pork meat for 24 hours
  2. Collect oil from boiling the meat and use to saute garlic and onion
  3. Add in meat and turn the heat up
  4. Pour in soy sauce until you hear your ancestors say: "Stop"
  5. Add vinegar in the same manner
  6. Pour water in until it gives out info about the rebels
  7. Cover up and let it simmer
  8. Salt to taste
